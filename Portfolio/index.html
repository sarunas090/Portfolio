About me

Greetings! I'm Giannis, a budding Web Developer with an unwavering passion for web design and development. Currently on the verge of completing my Bachelor of Science degree in Mathematics, I've discovered that my true calling lies in the world of coding and creativity.

Coding is my canvas, and I relish the art of crafting visually stunning and user-friendly websites. With a solid foundation in mathematics, I approach web development with a unique blend of analytical thinking and creative flair. Each project I undertake is an opportunity to weave logic and aesthetics into a seamless online experience.

Beyond coding, my life is enriched by the harmony of the Cretan Lyra and the graceful movements of folk Cretan dances. I don't just play the Lyra, I also share my passion for this traditional instrument as a tutor. Likewise, I offer my guidance to those who wish to explore the captivating world of dance. These cultural pursuits bring joy and depth to my life, just as they enrich my creativity in web development.

In addition to my pursuits in coding and the arts, I'm also an enthusiastic math tutor. I offer my expertise in mathematics, to eager learners. Education and mentorship are a crucial part of my journey, and I'm committed to helping others explore and excel in this area.

Above all, I'm thrilled about the opportunity to launch my career as a Web Developer. I'm eager to collaborate with fellow professionals, tackle new challenges, and continue my journey of growth and learning in the web development field.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I invite you to explore my work and the projects that showcase my dedication to creativity and innovation. If you're in search of a passionate and versatile Web Developer who's ready to bring your web projects to life, please don't hesitate to get in touch.

I look forward to the opportunity to collaborate with you and make the web a more beautiful and functional place, one project at a time.
